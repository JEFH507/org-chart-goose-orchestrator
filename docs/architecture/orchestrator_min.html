What is the Orchestrator?

The Orchestrator coordinates tasks across agents and tools while enforcing organization‑wide policies. It sits above individual agent instances and provides shared services such as routing, context brokering, and audit.

  • Directory & Policy — Central source of roles, groups, permissions, and global policies.
  • Task Router — Matches a task’s intent and constraints to the right agent(s) and tool(s).
  • Session Broker — Manages cross‑agent context handoff and continuity.
  • Audit & Observability — Captures events, approvals, errors, and metrics.
  • Profiles — Global and local recipes, memory, policies, hints, and ignore lists.
  • Policy Guardrails — Enforce privacy, data minimization, and approval checks.
  • Error Surface & Recovery — Normalizes errors so agents can self‑correct.
